Join Us

What you need to do & how to do it.

Basic Process

Step 1 · Make a decision

We do not have a fixed time for recruiting new members (But usually during the students in school), if you are interested in UIC HCC Computer Society, you can contact us immediately.

Know More about UIC HCC Computer Society

Step 2 · Make a reservation

If you want to join us, the first thing you should do is confirm the interview time with us by email (It is OK to use Chinese or English). We will reply as soon as we receive the email and confirm the final time with you.

Here is an example Email:
Recipients: UIC HCC Computer Society <hi@uichcc.com>
Subject: Apply for an interview
Message:
UIC HCC Computer Society:
Hello! My name is John Doe from Computer Science and Technology Major.
I am available every evening (6 PM-9 PM) of the next week (Sept.11 - Sept. 15).
Please tell me the specific interview time ASAP.

Thank you.
John Doe

Step 3 · Take the interview

You do not need to make extra preparations. Just attend the interview on time.

Step 4 · Waiting for results

After the interview, we will email the results to your email address. If you pass the interview, you will receive a form to collect information, please fill in carefully.

If you did not pass the interview, maybe we think you are not ready for UIC HCC Computer Society yet. You can make an appointment with us next time.
